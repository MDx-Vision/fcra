SECTION 1: DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES

Company agrees to provide the following consumer rights advocacy services:

A.    Review and analysis of Client's consumer credit reports from Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion.

B.    Identification of potentially inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable information.

C.    Preparation and submission of dispute letters to credit reporting agencies under FCRA § 1681i.

D.    Preparation and submission of debt validation requests under FDCPA § 1692g.

E.     Preparation of security freeze requests to secondary/specialty consumer reporting agencies.

F.     Correction of personal identifying information (PII) with credit bureaus.

G.    Preparation of method of verification (MOV) requests under FCRA § 1681i(a)(6).

H.    Preparation of regulatory complaints to CFPB, FTC, and State Attorneys General as warranted.

I.      Preparation of pre-arbitration demand letters when appropriate.

J.      Review and analysis of credit bureau responses.

K.    Ongoing consultation and case status updates.

SECTION 2: CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

A.    Client agrees to provide access to credit monitoring service credentials for report retrieval.

B.    Client agrees to forward all correspondence from credit bureaus, creditors, and collectors to Company within 5 business days of receipt.

C.    Client agrees to notify Company of any address, phone, or email changes within 5 business days.

D.    Client agrees to respond to Company inquiries and requests for information in a timely manner.

E.     Client acknowledges that delays in providing information may extend the service timeline.

4.2 Success Fee (Contingency on Monetary Recovery):

Company's compensation comes from successful outcomes. If Client receives any monetary settlement, judgment, or award as a result of violations identified through Company's services, Company shall receive 33% (thirty-three percent) of the gross monetary recovery.

This Success Fee is SEPARATE from and IN ADDITION TO the Cost Recovery Fees described above. The Success Fee represents Company's sole profit from the engagement.

Example: If Client pays $1,785 in Cost Recovery Fees during the case and receives a $15,000 settlement, Company receives an additional $4,950 (33%) as Success Fee. Client's net recovery would be $8,265.
