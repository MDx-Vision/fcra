SECTION 2: AGENT (ATTORNEY-IN-FACT)

IMPORTANT: This document authorizes consumer advocacy services only. Agent is NOT providing legal advice and is NOT acting as a licensed attorney. This Power of Attorney does not create an attorney-client relationship.

SECTION 3: SCOPE OF AUTHORITY

I authorize Agent to perform the following actions on my behalf:

3A. Credit Reporting Actions (FCRA § 1681 et seq.)

•       Dispute inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable information

•       Request reinvestigation of disputed items under § 1681i

•       Request method of verification (MOV) documentation under § 1681i(a)(6)

•       Correct personal identifying information (name, address, SSN, DOB)

•       Place, lift, or manage security freezes under § 1681c-1

•       Place or remove fraud alerts

•       Obtain copies of my consumer credit reports

•       Request disclosure of all information in my consumer file

•       Add consumer statements to credit file

3B. Debt Collection Actions (FDCPA § 1692 et seq.)

•       Send debt validation requests under § 1692g

•       Dispute debts and request verification

•       Request cease and desist of collection communications

•       Communicate with debt collectors regarding disputed accounts

3C. Data Furnisher Communications (FCRA § 1681s-2)

•       Dispute information directly with data furnishers

•       Request investigation of reported information

•       Demand correction or deletion of inaccurate information

3D. Regulatory Complaint Actions

•       File complaints with the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)

•       File complaints with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC)

•       File complaints with State Attorneys General offices

•       File complaints with state banking or consumer protection agencies

3E. Pre-Litigation and Arbitration Actions

•       Send pre-arbitration demand letters

•       Initiate arbitration proceedings as permitted by applicable agreements

•       Send intent-to-sue notifications

•       Negotiate pre-litigation settlements

3F. General Communications

•       Sign correspondence on my behalf

•       Receive and respond to communications

•       Request and receive documents and records

•       Access online portals and accounts related to credit monitoring services I have authorized

SECTION 5: HIPAA AUTHORIZATION FOR MEDICAL DEBT

To the extent that any disputed information relates to medical debt or medical services, I hereby authorize Agent to:

•       Request and receive protected health information (PHI) related to billing and debt collection

•       Communicate with healthcare providers, medical billing companies, and medical debt collectors

•       Dispute medical debt information appearing on credit reports

•       Request itemized statements and billing records

This HIPAA authorization complies with 45 CFR § 164.508 and shall remain valid for the duration of this Power of Attorney.

SECTION 6: DURATION AND TERMINATION

Duration: This Power of Attorney shall remain in full force and effect UNTIL REVOKED IN WRITING by the Principal.

Revocation: Principal may revoke this Power of Attorney at any time by providing written notice to Agent at the address listed above. Revocation shall be effective upon Agent's receipt of such notice.

Survival: Any actions taken by Agent in good faith prior to receiving notice of revocation shall remain valid and binding.

SECTION 7: LIMITATIONS AND DISCLAIMERS

1.     Agent is NOT authorized to practice law or provide legal advice.

2.     Agent is NOT authorized to file lawsuits on Principal's behalf.

3.     Agent is NOT authorized to accept service of process on Principal's behalf.

4.     Agent is NOT authorized to make financial decisions beyond those expressly stated herein.

5.     This Power of Attorney does not authorize Agent to incur debt or make financial commitments on Principal's behalf.

SECTION 8: INDEMNIFICATION

Principal agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Agent from any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from Agent's good-faith performance of duties authorized herein, except in cases of gross negligence or willful misconduct.

 

SECTION 9: EXECUTION

I, the undersigned Principal, being of sound mind and acting of my own free will, hereby execute this Limited Power of Attorney and authorize the Agent to act on my behalf as described herein.
