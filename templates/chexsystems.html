About ChexSystems/EWS

What are these bureaus?

ChexSystems tracks checking account history - closures, overdrafts, and unpaid balances.

Early Warning Services (EWS) is similar but used by different banks.

  • Reports stay for 5 years
  • Affects ability to open new bank accounts
  • Can dispute inaccurate information under FCRA
