STAGE 6

Understanding the Dispute Rounds

Round 1: Initial Disputes

  • We analyze your credit reports
  • Identify all disputable items
  • Send disputes to all three bureaus
  • Bureaus have 30-45 days to respond
Your job: Upload ALL responses you receive!

Round 2: Follow-Up Disputes

  • We analyze bureau responses
  • Items not deleted get re-disputed
  • Stronger legal language used
  • Different dispute strategies applied
Your job: Keep uploading responses!

Round 3: Escalated Disputes

  • Direct bureau communication
  • CFPB complaint preparation
  • Method of Verification (MOV) requests
  • Demand for documented proof
Your job: Continue uploading all mail!

Round 4: Pre-Litigation

  • Final demand letters
  • Attorney review (if applicable)
  • Settlement negotiation
  • Litigation preparation if needed
Your job: Stay responsive and upload everything!
