Credit Bureau Account Setup

Instructions for creating your dispute portal accounts

Why You Need These Accounts

To dispute inaccurate information on your credit reports, we need access to the online dispute portals at TransUnion, Experian, and Equifax.

Important: We cannot create these accounts on your behalf. The bureaus require identity verification through personal security questions that only you can answer, such as:

  • Which of these companies is your mortgage lender?
  • What is the monthly payment on your auto loan?
  • Which of these addresses have you lived at?

Once created, share your login credentials with us so we can submit disputes on your behalf.

What You'll Need

Item Details

Social Security Number Full 9 digits

1
TransUnion

Website: dispute.transunion.com

Instructions

  1. Go to dispute.transunion.com
  2. Click "Create Account" or "Sign Up"
  3. Enter your personal information
  4. Create a username and password
  5. Answer the security verification questions
  6. Verify your email address if prompted

If verification fails, you may need to verify by mail. TransUnion will send a verification code to your address within 5-7 business days.

TransUnion Credentials
2
Experian

Website: experian.com/disputes

Instructions

  1. Go to experian.com/disputes
  2. Click "Sign Up" or "Create Account"
  3. Enter your personal information
  4. Create username and password
  5. Answer security verification questions
  6. Enter verification code sent via text or email

If verification fails, Experian may require mail verification. They'll send a PIN to your address on file.

Experian Credentials
3
Equifax

Website: equifax.com/personal/credit-report-services

Instructions

  1. Go to equifax.com/personal/credit-report-services
  2. Click "Create a myEquifax Account"
  3. Enter your personal information
  4. Create username and password
  5. Answer security verification questions
  6. Complete ID.me verification if prompted (photo ID + selfie)

If verification fails, Equifax may require ID.me verification or mail verification.

Equifax Credentials

Security Guidelines

  • Do not email passwords in plain text
  • Do not share credentials via regular text message
  • Keep a copy of your credentials in a safe place
  • Do not log into the portals to submit disputes yourself
  • Contact us before attempting to reset if locked out

Troubleshooting

Problem Solution
Failed security questions Try again with different questions, or use mail verification
Account already exists Click "Forgot Password" to reset
Locked out Wait 24 hours or call the bureau
Verification code not received Check spam folder, wait 10 minutes, request again
