How Inquiry Disputes Work

This process removes unauthorized hard inquiries from your credit report under FCRA Section 604 (Permissible Purposes) and Section 609 (Disclosure).

When to Use:
  • Inquiries NOT attached to open accounts
  • Inquiries you did not authorize
  • Fraudulent/identity theft inquiries
Process:
  • File FTC Report (identity theft basis)
  • Submit CFPB complaints (per bureau)
  • Send certified 609 letters
Timeline:
  • Typical removal: 7-14 business days
  • CFPB response: 15 days required
  • Bureau investigation: 30 days max
