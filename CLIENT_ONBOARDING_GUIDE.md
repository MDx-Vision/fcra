### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

1. **Email Address** - Enter the email you registered with
2. **Password** - Enter your password
3. Click **Sign In**

### First-Time Login

If this is your first time logging in:
1. Use the temporary password sent to your email/SMS
2. You'll be prompted to create a new password
3. Choose a strong password (8+ characters, mix of letters and numbers)

### Forgot Password?

1. Click "Forgot password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Follow the link to create a new password

### Step 2: Select Document Type

When uploading, select what type of document it is:

| Type | When to Use |
|------|-------------|
| **Bureau Response** | Letter received from Equifax, Experian, or TransUnion |
| **Creditor Response** | Letter from a creditor (bank, collection agency) |
| **ID Verification** | Copy of ID, utility bill, or SSN card |
| **Supporting Evidence** | Payment records, account statements, etc. |
| **Other** | Any other relevant document |

### Step 3: Upload

1. Click **Browse Files** or drag and drop
2. Select the document type
3. Add a note (optional)
4. Click **Upload**

### Tips for Good Uploads

- **Scan or photograph clearly** - Make sure text is readable
- **Upload the full document** - Include all pages
- **Label correctly** - Choose the right document type
- **Upload promptly** - Don't wait to upload bureau responses

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account Questions

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to your profile (click your name in the top right) â†’ Change Password

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot password?" on the login page and follow the instructions.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Contact your case manager to update your email.

### Case Questions

**Q: How long does a dispute round take?**
A: Credit bureaus have 30-45 days to respond to disputes.

**Q: What happens after Round 1?**
A: We review the responses. If items weren't resolved, we proceed to Round 2 with stronger language.

**Q: How many rounds are there?**
A: Typically 2-4 rounds, depending on bureau responses.

**Q: What if the bureaus don't fix the errors?**
A: We may escalate to CFPB complaints or litigation.

### Document Questions

**Q: What documents should I upload?**
A: Upload any letters you receive from credit bureaus or creditors.

**Q: How do I know if my upload was successful?**
A: You'll see a confirmation message and the file will appear in your Documents list.

**Q: What file types are accepted?**
A: PDF, JPG, PNG files up to 10MB each.

### Letter Questions

**Q: Were my letters sent?**
A: Check the Letters tab - each letter shows its status (Draft, Ready, Sent).

**Q: How can I track my letters?**
A: Click "Track Delivery" next to any sent letter to see USPS tracking.

**Q: Can I get physical copies of my letters?**
A: Yes, contact your case manager to request printed copies.
