Credit Bureau Account Setup Instructions

WHY YOU NEED TO CREATE THESE ACCOUNTS

In order to dispute inaccurate information on your credit reports, we need access to the online dispute portals at each of the three major credit bureaus: TransUnion, Experian, and Equifax.

Important: We cannot create these accounts on your behalf because the bureaus require identity verification through personal security questions that only YOU can answer. These questions are based on your credit history and personal information - things like:
  • Which of these companies is your mortgage lender?
  • What is the monthly payment on your auto loan?
  • Which of these addresses have you lived at?
  • What year did you open your oldest credit card?

Since we don't know the answers to these personal questions, you must create the accounts yourself. Once created, you'll share the login credentials with us so we can submit disputes on your behalf.

STEP 1: CREATE TRANSUNION ACCOUNT

Website: https://dispute.transunion.com

Instructions:

  1. Go to dispute.transunion.com
  2. Click "Create Account" or "Sign Up"
  3. Enter your personal information (name, SSN, DOB, address)
  4. Create a username and password
  5. Answer the security verification questions
  6. Verify your email address if prompted
  7. Record your login credentials below
If verification fails: You may need to verify by mail. TransUnion will send a verification code to your address. This takes 5-7 business days.
TRANSUNION LOGIN CREDENTIALS

STEP 2: CREATE EXPERIAN ACCOUNT

Website: https://www.experian.com/disputes

Instructions:

  1. Go to experian.com/disputes
  2. Click "Sign Up" or "Create Account"
  3. Enter your personal information
  4. Create username and password
  5. Answer security verification questions
  6. You may receive a verification code via text/email
  7. Record your login credentials below
If verification fails: Experian may require mail verification. They'll send a PIN to your address on file.
EXPERIAN LOGIN CREDENTIALS

STEP 3: CREATE EQUIFAX ACCOUNT

Website: https://www.equifax.com/personal/credit-report-services

Instructions:

  1. Go to equifax.com/personal/credit-report-services
  2. Click "Create a myEquifax Account"
  3. Enter your personal information
  4. Create username and password
  5. Answer security verification questions
  6. Equifax may use ID.me for verification (photo ID + selfie)
  7. Record your login credentials below
If verification fails: Equifax may require ID.me verification (upload photo ID and take a selfie) or mail verification.
EQUIFAX LOGIN CREDENTIALS

AFTER YOU CREATE ALL 3 ACCOUNTS

Once you have successfully created accounts at all three bureaus, please share your login credentials with us using ONE of these secure methods:

  • Option 1: Fill out this form and return it to us via secure email or in person
  • Option 2: Call us and provide the information over the phone
  • Option 3: Send via encrypted message through our client portal

IMPORTANT SECURITY NOTES

  • Do NOT email passwords in plain text without encryption
  • Do NOT share credentials via regular text message
  • Keep a copy of your credentials in a safe place
  • Do NOT log into the portals and submit disputes yourself - we will handle this
  • If you get locked out, contact us before attempting to reset

TROUBLESHOOTING

Problem Solution
Failed security questions Try again - you get multiple attempts with different questions. If still failing, use mail verification.
Account already exists Click "Forgot Password" to reset. If email/phone changed, call bureau directly.
Locked out after too many attempts Wait 24 hours and try again, or call bureau for assistance.
Phone number not recognized Select mail verification option instead.
ID.me verification required (Equifax) Have photo ID ready. Take clear photo of ID front/back, then take selfie.
Verification code not received Check spam folder. Wait 10 minutes. Request code again.
